ATM Interface — Human-Centered Design Mockup

A working click-through prototype. Side dots simulate the physical function keys found on real ATMs, mapped live to the option beside them — so touch and hardware stay consistent. Try the accessibility controls below the screen.

Group 5 ATMSession: Guest
Design intent. Every screen keeps one primary action, large touch targets (≥48px), visible focus states for keyboard/assistive switches, and a confirm step before money moves. The accessibility bar simulates the kind of settings a real unit would expose at the start of a session (text size, contrast, and a screen-reader-style caption feed) rather than burying them in a menu.
